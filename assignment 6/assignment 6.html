Electronic

Electricity is the flow of charge in a close circult. Current is rate of flow, measured in Amps. Resistance is measure of opposition to current flow in an electrical circuit Measured in ohms. Voltage is the difference in electrical potential between two points, measured in Volts.

Ohm's Law

Ohm's Law states Current x resistance = voltage.

Finding the resistance in the circult

  1. Resistors in parallel, R = (1/R1 + 1/R2)^-1
  2. Resistors in series, R = R1 + R2
