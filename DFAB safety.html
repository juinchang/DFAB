DFAB safety

FabLab @ Singapore Polytechnic has a wide range of tools, equipment and machinery that I can use to make my project. Throughout this module, I will be taught on how to operate some of these tools and equipment. But this safety test teaches me how to be safe in the lab.

Emergency Procedures

  1. For police - call 999
  2. For ambulance - call 995
  3. For SCDF - call 995
  4. For all emergency and incident report - contact sp emergency hotline 6772-1234

Some FabLab Tools

  2. safety gloves

  4. safety soft-face hammer
