3D modelling

Firstly, I went to google to get a 2d image of the knight piece. Imported it into fusion 360, enlarge the image size, and sketch it. I sketched the horse first, then extruded it to about 4mm. For the base, its a bit tricky. I need to create two seperate sketches then revolve them together rather than revolving them seperately.

Well, things that I want to highlight to my fellow schoolmates is this. First, check the dimension of the image that you import. You do not want to do the model, only to find out that your model is very tiny.
